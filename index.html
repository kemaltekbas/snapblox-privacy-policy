Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

SnapBlox ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile puzzle game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use SnapBlox on your device.

1. Information We Collect

SnapBlox itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The only data stored is your local best score, which is saved on-device using your device's storage and never leaves your device.

2. Advertising

SnapBlox displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS, with your permission)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device type, OS version, and language settings
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

On iOS 14+, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier. You may opt out at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalized advertising, visit:
https://www.google.com/settings/ads

3. Data Storage

Your best score is stored locally on your device using Flutter's SharedPreferences. This data is never uploaded to any server and is removed when you uninstall the app.

4. Children's Privacy

SnapBlox is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5. Third-Party Services

The following third-party service is used in SnapBlox:

Google AdMob
Purpose: Display advertisements
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access or delete data held about you by our advertising partners
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw tracking consent at any time via iOS Settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
